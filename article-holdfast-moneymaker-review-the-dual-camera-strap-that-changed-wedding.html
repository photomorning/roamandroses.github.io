Holdfast MoneyMaker Review: The Dual Camera Strap That Changed Wedding Photography

By Felicia Bloom May 21, 2026

The Holdfast MoneyMaker has become a visual shorthand for professional wedding photography — and for good reason. This full-grain leather dual-camera harness distributes the weight of two bodies across your back, looks incredible doing it, and improves with every shoot as the leather molds to your body.

Key Features

  • Full-grain U.S. native steer hide leather
  • Stainless steel hardware (rust-proof)
  • Carries 2 cameras (optional 3rd with chest strap)
  • Reinforced nylon safety straps
  • Available in S, M, L sizes
  • Lifetime warranty (leather models)

Build Quality & Feel

The leather is thick, substantial, and occasionally features natural scarring that makes each strap one of a kind. Out of the box, it's stiff — wearing it feels like breaking in expensive leather boots. After consistent use, it softens and conforms to your body, eventually feeling like a second skin.

The stainless steel D-rings and sailboat clips are bombproof. You could — and Holdfast has demonstrated — do chin-ups from this strap.

Real-World Performance

The core value proposition is simple: you carry two cameras all day without neck, shoulder, or back strain. The strap redistributes weight across your upper back — the strongest part of your torso. Accessing cameras becomes an addictive one-handed motion: grab, slide up the strap, shoot.

The safety clips (an update from the original design) add redundancy, though most photographers will find the sailboat clips more than adequate on their own.

Variants

Camera Swagg (canvas): Lighter, more packable, breathes better in hot climates. Ideal for destination weddings.

Vegan: All-weather rubber-like material in 15 colors. Handles rain better than leather and won't discolor.

MoneyMaker Solo: Single-camera cross-body version with the same premium materials.

Skinny (1" straps): For smaller cameras or narrower shoulders. Removable shoulder pads included.

Practical Tips

  • Practice putting it on at home before your first shoot — it takes a few tries to feel natural.
  • Adjust the rear belt buckles so cameras hang about 4 inches below your armpits.
  • When walking through narrow aisles (weddings), pull cameras close or rotate them inward.
  • Use code SHOTKIT10 for 10% off.

FAQ

Q: Will the leather damage my clothes? The suede-like back sheds slightly at first. It won't stain clothes, but the leather can leave "sweat tramlines" on your shirt in hot weather just like any strap.

Q: Can I use a tripod with the MoneyMaker? Yes — the tripod mount screw stays in place as long as your plate is Arca-Swiss compatible. You may need to remove the screw to attach certain plates.

Q: Is it worth the price? For wedding and event photographers who carry two cameras daily, absolutely. This is a forever product that improves with age.
