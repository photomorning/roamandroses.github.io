# Kate Middleton Channels Back-to-School Elegance in a Tartan Dress

## FAQ

**Q: What brand was Kate Middleton's tartan dress?**

**Q: Why is tartan associated with British heritage?**
Tartan has deep roots in Scottish and British history, traditionally associated with clans, royalty, and formal occasions. It carries strong connotations of tradition, continuity, and national identity.
